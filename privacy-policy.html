Privacy Policy

Welcome to Find Near Room. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Room and roommate details submitted by users
  • Photos uploaded through forms
  • Location information (if permission is granted)
  • Device/browser information
  • Notification token for Firebase notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Display room and roommate posts
  • Allow communication between users
  • Improve website and app functionality
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Prevent spam and misuse

3. Permissions Used in Android App

Our Android app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission – To upload room photos
  • Storage Permission – To select images from gallery
  • Internet Permission – To access online services
  • Notification Permission – To send updates and alerts
  • Location Permission – To help users find nearby rooms

4. Firebase Notifications

We use Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send important notifications such as:

  • New room alerts
  • App updates
  • Announcements
  • Important service messages

5. Ads and Analytics

We may display advertisements using Google AdSense or other ad services. These services may use cookies or anonymous identifiers to improve ad experience.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, some information may be shared with trusted services:

  • Google Maps APIs
  • Firebase services
  • Image hosting services
  • Email delivery services

7. User Uploaded Content

Any room photos, roommate posts, or public information submitted by users may be visible to other users on the platform.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no online system can guarantee 100% security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
