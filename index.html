How Find Near Room Works

The goal is to make the process of finding or posting a room as simple as possible for students, working professionals and families.

1. Fill a simple form

Choose whether you need a room, want to post a room, or are searching for a roommate. Fill basic details like area, budget and gender preference.

2. Get visible instantly

Your post becomes visible on our room / roommate page so that interested people can contact you directly on your phone number.

3. Filter and shortlist

Use filters for location, budget, room type and gender to quickly shortlist rooms that match your lifestyle and price range.

4. Talk & finalise directly

We don’t charge brokerage. Final discussion, visit and confirmation happens directly between owner and tenant/roommate.
