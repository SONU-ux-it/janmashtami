Who we are

Built for practical room searching

Find Near Room is a local-first platform created to help students, job seekers, and working professionals find or post rooms for rent without confusion or heavy brokerage.

Many people move to a new city and struggle to find a safe, affordable place to stay. Find Near Room tries to solve this real problem by providing a simple website where owners and tenants can connect directly.

What this website does:

  • Shows rooms, PGs and hostels with clear details like budget, facilities and location.
  • Allows owners to post their rooms so that genuine tenants can contact them directly.
  • Helps students and professionals search by budget, gender and room type from a single page.
Our story

Why this platform was started

The idea of Find Near Room started after noticing how difficult it is for students and freshers to find a room in a new city. Many people depend only on brokers or random social media groups, which often leads to confusion, extra costs and wasted time.

Instead of building a very complex system, this platform focuses on one thing: making it easy to see multiple room options with clear contact details, and helping owners reach interested people quickly.

Less confusion

Important details are easier to check before you call or visit a property.

Less dependency

The goal is to reduce over-reliance on brokers and scattered social media posts.

More clarity

Users should quickly understand room type, rent range, and who the listing suits.

Vision and mission

What we want to build

Vision: To become a trusted local platform where anyone can easily discover rooms, PGs and roommates in their city without feeling lost or cheated.

Mission: To keep the process of listing and searching rooms simple, transparent and friendly – especially for students, working professionals and small families.

  • Encourage direct connection between owner and tenant.
  • Highlight important details like rent, deposit and room facilities.
  • Keep the website lightweight and mobile-friendly for quick use.
Website actions

What you can do on this website

On Find Near Room you can:

  • Check Available Rooms & PGs with filters for budget, gender and room type.
  • Post your own room if you have a vacant room, flat or PG to rent out.
  • Search for a roommate if you already have a flat and just need someone to share the rent.
  • Read helpful guides and tips on the home page before finalising any place.
Values

The principles behind the project

Some simple values guide this project:

  • Simplicity: Forms, pages and content are kept clean so that anyone can use the site easily, even on a basic smartphone.
  • Transparency: Effort is made to show clear rent, deposit and room facilities so that users know what to expect before visiting.
  • Local Focus: Instead of spreading everywhere at once, the focus stays on doing a good job for a few cities and areas first.
